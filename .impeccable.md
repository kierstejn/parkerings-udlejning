## Design Context

### Users
Danish landlords and renters. Landlords list and manage parking spaces; renters browse and book. Used primarily on desktop and mobile, in a practical context — not leisure browsing.

### Brand Personality
Trusted, practical, Nordic. A serious property platform — closer to a Danish boligportal than a lifestyle brand. Competent, no-nonsense, with a subtle warmth.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Clean, professional, structured. Think refined Scandinavian utility — nothing gratuitous, everything intentional. Reference: Danish property platforms and Nordic SaaS tools. Anti-reference: anything that looks like a startup pitch deck or AI dashboard.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity over cleverness — information hierarchy is everything
2. Restraint signals trust — unnecessary decoration undermines credibility
3. Functional beauty — every visual choice earns its place by improving usability
4. Nordic restraint — generous whitespace, muted palette, sharp type
5. Respect the user's time — dense but not crowded; fast to scan
